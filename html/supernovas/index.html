Super Novas - Nova Labs Program for Young Makers

'Super Novas' is our program for younger kids (roughly ages 3-10). It is intended to have a periodic organized activity for kids with a focus on Maker interests (DIY, Arts and Crafts, Inventing, Science, etc). We are also working on a youth membership program that would allow kids ages 10-18 to become Youth Members at Nova Labs and have supervised access to the regular Nova Labs activities, tools and equipment.

If you want to be informed when we have new kids activities available then you have these options:

  • Watch the wiki page at http://www.nova-labs.org/wiki/supernovas for updates as the Super Novas program progresses.
  • Join Nova Labs and then Sign up for the Kids Mailing List.
  • Join our Meetup group to be notified when new events are scheduled. (Note that this will notify you for all new events, not just kids activities.)
