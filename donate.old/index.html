Support Nova Labs!

There are currently two important ways to support Nova Labs financially: as a Regular Contributor or as a Donor.

A Regular Contributor supports Nova Labs by making recurring contributions on a regular schedule, typically monthly. This allows us to budget for expenses and plan for growth. This is recommended for supporters who plan to utilize the space and participate in activities on a regular basis. We are committed to supporting the community by promoting making for all, regardless of someone's ability to pay. While a contribution is not required to participate in our activities, for those who are able, we suggest a support level of $50 per month.

Donors who believe in and support our mission but who may not have the opportunity to utilize our space on a regular basis can show their support for Nova Labs by making one-time or less frequent donations. Donations can be used to support capital costs like new equipment and are vital for providing an enriching experience for our community of makers.
