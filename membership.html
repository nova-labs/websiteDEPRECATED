Nova Labs Membership

Nova Labs encourages a diverse and connected community through involved participants.

At Nova Labs we're excited to be building a high-quality community of involved Makers. We want to encourage everyone to be as involved as you're able. Everyone is welcome to join any of our public events and activities, and to use our facilities during open hours. If you would like to become a full member, then you need to complete a Maker Quest and apply through your Sponsor.

Please direct questions to your Sponsor or membership at nova-labs dot org

Membership Levels at Nova Labs

Associate Members

Dues Suggested $50 per month
Space Access During open hours and events
Tool and Equipment Access First come basis
Eligibility Everyone!
This level was designed to accommodate members who may not be able to spend a lot of time, or are students with other expenses. See our schedule online or coordinate with your Sponsor to attend events and get involved.

Full Members

Dues $100 per month
Space Access 24x7
Tool and Equipment Access Priority use
Eligibility Associate Members only
Our members are an integral part of our community. They take on responsibilities in addition to completing projects and sponsoring other up-and-coming members. There is no checklist for getting through this process - talk to your Sponsor if you're interested in full membership.

How to Arrange Monthly Dues and Contributions

Paper Check

Make a check out to 'Nova Labs', fill in the memo line, and place the check in our drop safe located on the lobby counter.

Electronic (Recurring) Payments

See your account page to setup recurring contributions via Amazon Payments.

Open Houses

Every Second Thursday of each Month at 7PM

Come to Nova Labs! See facilities, meet members, view projects.

Membership and Safety Orientation

Every Other Wednesday at 7:30PM

Orientation is required before using the shop tools. The course keeps you, your neighbors, and the machine tools safe.

Nova Labs is committed to maintaining a safe, shared environment for everyone.

Events

All events are scheduled through Meetup.com - Nova Makers via Meetup
